The English established their first factory in Bengal in 1651 at____

(A) ** Hugli

(B) Kassimbazar

(C) Patna

(D) Calcutta

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first factory of British was established at Hugli in 1651. It was abandoned in 1690 when they shifted to Calcutta.

Concept note-2: -The first English factory was set up on the banks of the river Hugli in Bengal 1651. The East India Company convinced merchants and traders to come and settle near the factory. By 1696, the Company began building a fort around the Hugli settlement.

Concept note-3: -In Which Year was the First English Factory Set Up on the Banks of the River Hooghly? The first English factory was founded in 1651 on the banks of the river Hooghly in Bengal. The East India Company began trading in Bengal.

Concept note-4: -The first English factory in India was established at Surat. By January 1613, the first East India Company factory had come up at Surat.
