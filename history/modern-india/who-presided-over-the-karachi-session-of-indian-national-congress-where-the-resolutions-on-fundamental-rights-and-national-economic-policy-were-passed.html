Who presided over the Karachi Session of Indian National Congress where the resolutions on Fundamental Rights and National Economic Policy were passed?

(A) Maulana Abul Kalam Azad

(B) Jawahar Lal Nehru

(C) ** Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel

(D) Subhash Chandra Bose

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru. Pandit Jawahar Lal Nehru has drafted the resolution of Fundamental Rights and economic program. The Karachi Congress Session which was held on March 26 to 31, 1931 was chaired by Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel.

Concept note-2: -The Karachi Resolution was passed by the Indian National Congress at its Karachi session in the year 1931. Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel was the president of the session. The resolutions on Fundamental Rights and National Economic Policy were passed in that session.

Concept note-3: -The Karachi Session of the Congress in 1931 passed the resolution on the Fundamental Rights. 3. The Congress Session of Lucknow in 1936 was held under the Presidentship of Subhash Chandra Bose. 4.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Karachi Session 1931. The resolution on Fundamental Rights was adopted by the Congress at its Karachi Session (1931).
