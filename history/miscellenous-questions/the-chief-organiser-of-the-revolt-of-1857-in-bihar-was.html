(A) Khan Bahadur Khan

(B) ** Kunwar Singh

(C) Rao Sahib

Concept note-2: -In 1857, Kunwar Singh led the Bihar uprising. The revolts took place in Delhi under Colonial Bhukth Khan, in Kanpur under Nana Saheb and Tantya Tope, in Lucknow under Begum Hazrat Mahal, in Jhansi under Lakshmi Bai, in Bareilly under Khan Bahadur Khan, and in Bihar under Kunwar Singh.
