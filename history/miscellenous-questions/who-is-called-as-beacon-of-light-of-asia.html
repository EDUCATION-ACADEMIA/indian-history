Who is called as “beacon of light of Asia”

(A) ** Subhash Chandra Bose

(B) Mahatma Gandhi

(C) Gautam Buddha

(D) Abdul Gaffer khan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Subhash Chandra Bose is prominently known as ‘Beacon of Light of Asia’ and he is also known as “Netaji".

Concept note-2: -Gautama Buddha. founder of Buddhism is considered ‘The Light of Asia’.

Concept note-3: -In 1942, he earned the title ‘Netaji’, in Germany by the Indian soldiers of the Azad Hind Fauj. He was a very great and brave leader of the country, who was famous for his stringent struggle in the name of Netaji.

Concept note-4: -Even though Bose and Mahatma Gandhi had differing ideologies, the latter called Bose the “Patriot of Patriots” in 1942.

Concept note-5: -In 1942, Gandhi ji called Bose the “Prince among the Patriots”. Gandhi ji said that Netaji’s “patriotism is second to none. His bravery shines through all his actions.
