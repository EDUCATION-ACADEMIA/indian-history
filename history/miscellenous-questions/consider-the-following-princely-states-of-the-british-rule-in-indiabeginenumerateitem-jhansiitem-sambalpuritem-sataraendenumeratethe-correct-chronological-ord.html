Consider the following Princely States of the British rule in India:

1. :

Jhansi

2. :

Sambalpur

3. :

Satara

The correct chronological order in which, they were annexed by the British is:

(A) ** 3-2-1

(B) 1-3-2

(C) 1-2-3

(D) 3-1-2

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Correct Answer is Option 2, i.e Satara-Udaipur-Jhansi.

Concept note-2: -Annexation in the absence of a natural or adopted heir was enforced in the cases of Satara (1848), Jaitpur and Sambalpur (1849), Baghat (1850), Chota Udaipur (1852), Jhansi (1853), and Nagpur (1854).

Concept note-4: -The first state to be annexed under Doctrine of lapse was Satara (1848) followed by Jaipur and Sambalpur (1849), Bhagat (1850), Udaipur (1852), Jhansi (1853), Nagpur (1854).

Concept note-5: -Lord Dalhousie annexed Satara in 1848 and Nagpur and Jhansi in 1854 by applying doctrine of Lapse.
