With reference to the Revolt of 1857, who of the following laid siege to Jhansi in March 1858?

(A) Major General Wyndham

(B) Major-General Havelock

(C) ** Sir Hugh Rose

(D) Sir Colin Campbell

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hugh Rose, commanding the British forces, demanded the surrender of the city. After a fierce battle, Jhansi was fallen to the British army.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. Rani Laxmibai led the revolt of 1857 in Jhansi. Rani Laxmibai of Jhansi is also known by the name of ‘Manikarnika’. She led the revolt with the help of Tantia Tope.

Concept note-3: -’ In March 1858, the British troops arrived in Jhansi and laid siege to the city.

Concept note-4: -The regiment was named in honor of Rani Lakshmibai, the warrior queen of Jhansi who fought against British colonial rule in India in 1857.

Concept note-5: -Tantia Tope, also spelled Tatya Tope or Tantia Topi, original name Ramchandra Panduranga, (born c. 1813–19, Pune, India-died April 18, 1859, Shivpuri), a leader of the Indian Mutiny of 1857–58.
