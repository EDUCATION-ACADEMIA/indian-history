Which of the following was not one of the Raja Rammohan Roy’s suggestions for organising the Company’s government on a more rational basis?

(A) ** Constitutional reforms must be guaranteed

(B) Civil liberties and individual rights must be guaranteed

(C) The rule of law ought to be supreme

(D) The absolute ownership of property by the individual must be recognised

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Abolition of sati is the correct answer. Option B. Indianisation of Superior services was the demand of the Indian National Congress. It is an incorrect option.

Concept note-2: -He wanted the people of India to develop a sense of self confidence, and was crusader against unreason and superstition. He admired the English people who not only enjoyed civil and political liberty but was interested in promoting freedom, social happiness and rationalism in the areas where their influence extended.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is A, B, and C. Raja Rammohan Roy founded Atmiya Sabha in 1815 in Kolkata. He als0o founded Brahmo Samaj in 1828 in Kolkata. He also wrote a book named; A gift to Monotheists’ in 1804.

Concept note-4: -Crusaded against Hindu customs such as sati, polygamy, child marriage and the caste system. Demanded property inheritance rights for women. In 1828, he set up the Brahmo Sabha a movement of reformist Bengali Brahmins to fight against social evils.
