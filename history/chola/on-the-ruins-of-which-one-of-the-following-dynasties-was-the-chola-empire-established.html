On the ruins of which one of the following dynasties was the Chola empire established?

(A) Chalukyas of Venge

(B) ** Pallavas of Kanchi

(C) Pandyas of Madurai

(D) Chalukyas of Kalyani

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -1) The Chola empire was established on the ruins of the Pallava dynasty of Kanchi.

Concept note-2: -Tamil dynasties ( ) are the kingdoms who ruled over present day Tamil Nadu, Sri Lanka, Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, Kerala and Odisha. These include the Pallavas, the Pandyas, the Cholas and the Cheras.

Concept note-3: -Origin of the Chola Dynasty The reign of the Cholas began in the 9th century when they defeated the Pallavas to come into power. This rule stretched over for over five long centuries until the 13th century. However, around the 2nd century, the state Andhra has a Chola kingdom that flourished far and wide.

Concept note-4: -The founder of the Chola Empire was Vijayalaya, who was first feudatory of the Pallavas of Kanchi. He captured Tanjore in 850 A.D. He established a temple of goddess Nishumbhasudini (Durga) there. Aditya I succeeded Vijayalaya.

Concept note-5: -Hence, from the above-mentioned points, it becomes clear that Chola ruler Aditya I captured Kanchi defeating Pallavas.
