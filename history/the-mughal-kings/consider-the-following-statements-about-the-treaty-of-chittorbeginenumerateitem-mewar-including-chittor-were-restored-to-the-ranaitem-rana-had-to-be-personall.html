Consider the following statements about the Treaty of Chittor

1. :

Mewar, including Chittor were restored to the Rana.

2. :

Rana had to be personally present at the Mughal Court.

3. :

Rana could not repair or re-fortify chittor.

Which of the above statements is/are correct?

(A) ** 1 and 3 only

(B) 1, 2 and 3

(C) 1 and 2 only

(D) 1 only

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mewar, including Chittor were restored to the Rana, Rana could not repair or re-fortify chittor. Hence, the correct answer is option (A).

Concept note-2: -Treaty in 1615 In the treaty, it was agreed that: The ruler of Mewar, will not be bound to present himself in person at Mughal court, instead, a relative of the Rana would wait upon the Mughal Emperor and serve him. It was also agreed that the Ranas of Mewar would not enter matrimonial relations with the Mughals.

Concept note-3: -The Treaty of Chittor (1615 AD) was signed between the Mughal Emperor Jahangir and Rana Amar Singh of Mewar.

Concept note-4: -In 1616, after a treaty between Jahangir and Amar Singh, Chittorgarh was given back to Amar Singh by Jahangir on the condition that it would never be repaired because the Mughals were wary of the fort being used as a place of rebellion against them.

Concept note-5: -Mughal-Mewar treaty was signed between Jahangir and Amar Singh on 5th February 1615.
