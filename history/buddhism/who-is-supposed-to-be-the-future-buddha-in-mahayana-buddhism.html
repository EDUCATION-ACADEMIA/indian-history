Who is supposed to be the future Buddha in Mahayana Buddhism?

(A) Krakuchanda

(B) Amitabha

(C) Kanak Muni

(D) ** Maitreya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Maitreya is the Buddha of the future, who will be born to teach enlightenment in the next age. According to Mahayana teachings, a Buddha is first born as a bodhisattva, and then after many lifetimes, progresses on to Buddhahood. The historical Buddha was himself referred to as a bodhisattva before becoming the Buddha.

Concept note-2: -Maitreya, the messianic bodhisattva characterized as the Buddha of the Future, stands in a graciously exaggerated posture, the body beautifully counterbalanced.
