The ancient city of Raj Griha which is modern Rajgir near Patna was built by which of the following kings?

(A) ** Bimbisara

(B) Ajatshatru

(C) Prasenjit

(D) Ashoka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rajgir was the capital of Haryanka dynasty kings Bimbisara (558–491 BC) and Ajatashatru (492–460 BC). Ajatashatru kept his father Bimbisara in captivity here. The sources do not agree on which of the Buddha’s royal contemporaries, Bimbisara and Ajatashatru, was responsible for its construction.

Concept note-2: -It is said that the city of Rajagriha was built by Bimbisara himself. The city was covered on all sides by five hills creating a natural fortification, and later on Bimbisara’s son, Ajatashatru covered the gaps with stone walls.

Concept note-3: -Bimbisara was the first ruler of the Haryanka Dynasty. He was an excellent administrator and he ruled from 544 BCE to 492 BCE.

Concept note-4: -Bimbisara established the first capital of Magadha at Rajagriha. He ran a well administered kingdom, and Magadha prospered under him.
